spaces in mind,
filled with spice of love;
or poison of loneliness;
who knows?
all we know is what
that facial mirror expresses!
it sometimes feels
awful to be incapable to step inside
those secret memoirs
present inside those head spaces,

that
i wish i could
just uncover;
heartbroken and unhappy
i still cry
wishing that someone
could just utter that cancerous truth
haunting my sleeps and peace...
BY prabhdeep singh
"secrets!"
